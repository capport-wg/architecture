Issue: Provisioning Domains vs. RFC 7710

Do we let PvD serve the role previously described by RFC 7710?

Issue: RFC 7710

RFC 7710 is incompletely specified. Depending on adoption of PvD, either:
  • update RFC 7710, or
  • deprecate RFC 7710.

Issue: Indicate No Captive Portal

  • Would be nice to optimize network attachment if there is no captive portal.
  • RFC 7710 did not provide such a method.
